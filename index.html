Controls

Shortcuts
Press + to add a character.
Click on a players name to open a menu of actions.
Gold Controls
Press g to set gold values.
Left click gold count to subtract gold, right click to add.
Shift modifies balance by 50, a normal click by 10, and a control click by 5.
Stamina Controls
Press s to set max stamina values, middle click stamina to full heal.
Click to remove 1 stamina, shift click to remove 10 stamina, control click to remove 5 stamina.
Right click to add 1 stamina, shift+right click to add 10 stamina, ctrl+right click to add 5 stamina.
Gem Controls
Left click to remove gem, right click to temporarily add.
Press R to reset gems for the round, press L or reset gems for the combat encounter.
Anytimes Controls
Left click to remove an anytime, middle click to permanently add an anytime.
Press L to restore anytimes.
More anytime controls can be found by clicking a players name.
Limited Controls
Add limiteds in the player menu.
Press L to reset limiteds.
Hover over a limited gem to see the card it's attached to.

About this project

This is an open source project to assist people running campaigns using the urealms roleplaying engine. You can check out the source code and contribute to the project on github.
